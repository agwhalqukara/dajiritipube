Matt LeBlanc (TV Actor) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Matthew Steven LeBlanc is an American actor and comedian. He has gained worldwide recognition for his role as Joey Tribbiani in the NBC sitcom Friends and its Joey sub-series. For his work Friends, Le Blanc has received three Primetime Emmy Award nominations. He also played a fictional version of himself in Episodes, which won him the Golden Globe award and received four additional Emmy nominations. He co-hosted Top Gear from 2016 to 2019. From 2016 to 2020, he played Patriarch Adam Burns in the CBS sitcom Man with a Plan.
