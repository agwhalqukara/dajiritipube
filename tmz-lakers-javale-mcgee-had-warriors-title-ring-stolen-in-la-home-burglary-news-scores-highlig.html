TMZ: Lakers' JaVale McGee Had Warriors Title Ring Stolen in LA Home Burglary | News, Scores, Highlig

Andrew D. Bernstein/Getty Images

TMZ Sports reported McGee's home in L.A. was broken into Saturday. The thieves stole a pair of safes from the home that contained cash, one championship ring and the goggles he used during a locker-room celebration. The total value of the items and cash stolen reportedly exceeds $200,000.

No arrests have been made, nor have any suspects been named. However, police are reviewing surveillance footage from the home.

McGee spent two seasons in the Bay Area, winning back-to-back championships with the Golden State Warriors in 2017 and 2018. After making just one start in 16 postseason appearances in 2017, he took on a bigger role during the 2018 postseason, starting nine of his 13 appearances.

He averaged 8.0 points and 2.3 rebounds during the 2018 NBA Finals against the Cleveland Cavaliers.
