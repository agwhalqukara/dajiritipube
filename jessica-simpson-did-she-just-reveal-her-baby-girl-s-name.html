Jessica Simpson Did She Just Reveal Her Baby Girls Name?

Did Jessica Simpson just reveal the name she is planning to give her yet to be born baby girl? After the woman took to social media to share a pic from her baby shower, fans are convinced she also shared her child’s moniker.

In the snap, the pregnant singer and fashion designer poses alongside daughter Maxwell, 6, and behind them, there is a sign that reads: Birdie’s Nest!

Not only that but Simpson also wrote the same thing in the caption.

https://www.instagram.com/p/Bs1inj3jxZ5/

Her followers were quick to start guessing that Birdie is the name the mom chose for her unborn baby, which, if it’s true, it would be really cute.

‘Do we think the babie’s name will be Birdie?!’ one fan commented under the snap.

Another also thought the same and mentioned that they just loved that name for a baby girl.

As you may remember, the star shared the news that she was expecting a daughter back in September.

This will be her and husband Eric Johnson’s third addition to the family after big sister Maxwell and big brother Ace.

Simpson was over the moon at her baby shower which took place only one day after she told her followers that she’d found the solution for her extremely swollen feet – cupping!

The future mother of three had been dealing with the symptom for months!

During an interview with ET back in October, Simpson revealed how her other two kids reacted to the news that they were going to become big siblings.
