Lauren Graham Biography

Lauren Graham is best known as Actress who has an estimated Net Worth of $15 Million. Became known to TV audiences as Lorelai Gilmore on the CW series Gilmore Girls and as Sarah Braverman on Parenthood. She has also appeared in such films as Bad Santa, Evan Almighty and It's Kind of a Funny Story.  She was born on March 16,1967, Honolulu, HI. She used to do public appearances as Striker, the dog mascot of the US-based 1994 FIFA World Cup. In 2013, she released her first novel, entitled Someday, Someday, Maybe: A Novel.  She rode horses competitively as a young girl.  She co-starred with Alexis Bledel on the hit series Gilmore Girls from 2000 to 2007.  She dated her Parenthood co-star Peter Krause in 2010. She also previously dated Friends star Matthew Perry.  Her zodiac sign is Pisces.
