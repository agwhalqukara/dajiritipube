Hugh Grant Expecting Fourth Child, His Second With Anna Eberstein

Congrats are once again in order for Hugh Grant! The actor is expecting his fourth child, Entertainment Tonight confirmed on Monday, Oct. 12.

This will be the second child for the British actor, 55, and Swedish television producer Anna Eberstein, and Grant’s fourth child in four years.

The Notting Hill star is father to daughter Tabitha, 4, and son Felix, 2, with ex-girlfriend Tinglan Hong. He and Elberstein are already parents to John Mungo, 3.

Though Grant and Eberstein are not married nor confirmed to be dating, the two are very close.

“We are very good friends and she’s a very good mother,” the Bridget Jones’ Diary actor told Swedish newspaper Nojesbladet in September 2014.
