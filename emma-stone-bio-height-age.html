Emma Stone Bio, Height, Age

Who Is Emma Stone?

Emma Stone is an American actress. She got into acting when she was young and landed her first role in a production called The Wind in the Willows in 2000. Emma is best known for her roles in Superbad, Zombieland, Easy A and The Amazing Spider-Man film series.

She won several awards for her stupendous performance including an Academy Award, Golden Globe and BAFTA among others. She is one of the most successful actresses in Hollywood.

Short bio (Parents, Siblings)

Emily Jean Stone was born on the 6th of November 1988, in Scottsdale, Arizona, USA. Emma Stone is the older daughter of Krista Jean Stone and Jeffrey Charles Stone. She has a younger brother, Spencer Stone. Her paternal grandfather is Swedish by the origin and so is her father.

Her mother is of Scottish, German, English and Irish ancestry. Stone attended Xavier College Preparatory‍ but dropped out after studying for one semester to pursue her career in Film Industry.
