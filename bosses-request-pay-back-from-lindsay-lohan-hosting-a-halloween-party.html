Bosses Request Pay Back From Lindsay Lohan Hosting a Halloween Party

Officials at Foxwoods Resort and Casino in Connecticut have asked Lindsay Lohan to pay back the $50,000 she was paid to host a Halloween party because she showed up late. Lohan appeared at Foxwoods with Floyd Mayweather Jr. to judge a costume contest together. The report indicates that she was suppose to show up at 10 PM, but didn’t make it until around midnight. She also skipped the Red Carpet entrance she was suppose to be a part of.  Currently, bosses at the casino are looking to renegotiate the contract because of Lohan’s tardiness. There’s little chance they will succeed, but they are looking into it.

In other interesting news there are several photos of Lohan and Mayweather being nice and cozy at the party. There are several photos at The Huffington Post that show Lohan and Mayweather all hugged up. Sources in the report say the two were inseparable the entire night and spent time afterwards in the VIP room. There’s nothing more to report at this time on that front, but we will be sure to keep you updated. Also, for those of you wondering about Lohan’s recovery from substance abuse,  all reports of the night indicate that Lohan is clean despite the fact she still loves to party.
