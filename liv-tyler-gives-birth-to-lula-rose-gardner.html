Liv Tyler Gives Birth to Lula Rose Gardner

Liv Tyler and her fiancé Dan Gardner have welcomed a baby girl, named, Lula Rose.  

The model took to Instagram to share the happy news while sharing the first shot of the newborn baby being embraced by her daddy.

“Hello!!!!! our beautiful baby girl is here !!!!” she captioned the shot. “Lula Rose Gardner we are sooooo happy heart exploding with love !!!!!”

This is the second child for the couple.

The pair are also parents to 1-year-old son Sailor Gene.

Tyler is also parent to 11-year-old son, Milo, with ex-husband, Royston Langdon.
