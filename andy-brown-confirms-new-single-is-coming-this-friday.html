Andy Brown Confirms New Single is Coming This Friday

Ever since Andy Brown went solo with his country infused debut single ‘Landslide‘ featuring The Shires’ Crissie Rhodes, we have been totally obsessed with his voice. And we have been counting down the days until the release of his upcoming solo album which will be dropping in the second quarter of this year.

But while we still have a few months to wait for the release of the former Lawson front-man’s debut solo LP, we will be getting a brand new single from the the singer who recently became a dad for the first time to baby Knox with wife Joey, this Friday.

The rock/pop star turned country singer will be releasing a brand new track on Friday, January 19th titled #TOTT. So far Andy Brown has shared the name of the song, and has confirmed the release date, and he now shared some lyrics from his new release.

New music coming this Friday. Hope you dig. Love this one. #TOTT pic.twitter.com/sNqhx2McNF

Along with the release of #TOTT this Friday, Andy Brown will also be embarking on his first ever solo tour next month. Tickets for the shows are on sale now!

Make sure you follow Andy Brown on Twitter @AndyBrownMusic to keep up to date with all his latest music and tour news!
