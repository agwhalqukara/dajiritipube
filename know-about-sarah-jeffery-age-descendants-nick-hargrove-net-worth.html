Published Date: 05 Aug, 2019 Updated date: 05 Aug, 2019 By Celebs Podium

Sarah Jeffrey seems very close to her Charmed co-star, Nick Hargrove. The two keep on appearing on the Instagram pictures together. With that, fans are questioning whether the two are dating or not!

Acclaimed as an actress and singer, Sarah Jeffery is known for her work on the NBC series, Shades of Blue and the Descendants franchise. She is involved in the showbiz since 2013.

Sarah Jeffery Short Bio: Age, Height

The actress, age 22, was born with the name Sarah Marie Jeffery on 3 April 1996. She hails from her hometown of Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. She shares a sibling bond with her sister, Hannah Jeffery.

Standing at a decent height of 5 feet and 3 inches (1.6 m), Sarah has been dancing and acting in musicals and theatrical productions since she was three years old. She had a very successful career as a child.

Career, Net Worth

Sarah Jeffery has a commanding net worth of 500K. Most of her earnings are the result of her lead role of Maggie Vera on the CW fantasy-drama series, Charmed (2018-present). Sarah Jeffery as Maggie Vera on Charmed (Picture: IMDB)
