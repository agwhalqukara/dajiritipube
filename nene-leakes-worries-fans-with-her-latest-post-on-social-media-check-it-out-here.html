NeNe Leakes Worries Fans With Her Latest Post On Social Media Check It Out Here

NeNe Leakes is usually happy, and she jokes all the time but, more than once, she said that this is just a coping mechanism for her when she’s going through something difficult. And what more difficult situation could she be facing these days than caring for her beloved husband who has been diagnosed with cancer.

There are more terrible situations in life; we know, but this must be really hard on the RHOA star these days and her followers, agree – especially the ones who know what it’s like to be a caretaker.

NeNe’s recent message on Instagram has fans worried about her.

‘Gotta laugh to keep from crying’ NeNe captioned a photo of herself in which she’s laughing.

Her fans showed her nothing but support in the comments section.

Someone told her ‘you May not read this. But, I understand how it feels to literally fear for your husband’s life. It’s the most debilitating feeling in the world, yet you can not show it because you have to be strong for him. I would usually have my crying sessions in the shower so he wouldn’t hear me. Please know that I am praying for you, that God will continue to give you strength, but most of all that he comfort you in your time of need, that he will hear your cries and prayer and heal your hubby, and that he also protect your family and marriage!’
