Anthony Jeselnik Announces Date for Comedy Central Series

Anthony Jeselnik took to Twitter to announce the premiere date for his new Comedy Central series Good Talk with Anthony Jeselnik.

Here's a press release with all the information I announced on Friday. I was supposed to wait until today, but I think it's funny when publicity people make a bunch of plans and then I ruin them.https://t.co/bRSreVVPFd

— Anthony Jeselnik (@anthonyjeselnik) August 26, 2019

Set to air on September 6 at 11 p.m. ET/PT, Good Talk with Anthony Jeselnik will center around Jeselnik interviewing some of his friends, who also happen to be some of the biggest names in comedy.

According to the press release provided on Twitter, “Over the course of six episodes, Jeselnik sits down with Nick Kroll, Natasha Leggero, Kumail Nanjiani, Tig Notaro, Kristen Schaal and David Spade.”

It adds:

In Good Talk, Jeselnik switches gears from his signature acerbic style for tongue-in-cheek interviews with his fellow comedians about their careers, influences and what it’s like to live in his massive shadow. Jeselnik and his guests center their extremely funny conversations around stand up, stories and bits and participate in elegantly bizarre studio segments.

Within the same press release, it was also announced that Jeselnik’s hilarious podcast will be back for a second season through Comedy Central’s Global Podcast Network. The Jeselnik and Rosenthal Vanity Project is set to return on September 4 and will be available wherever you listen to podcasts.

Both Jeselnik and Rosenthal have put in tremendous amounts of work into the podcast, including a live podcast in front of the folks at Clusterfest earlier this year.
