Britney Spears Squeaks in Leather in &quot;Oops I Did It Again!&quot; Rendition

You see her problem is this: She’s squeaking away! A musicless rendition of Britney Spears‘ “Oops I Did It Again” video posted this week, re-imagines the pop superstar squeaking away in her famous leather costume from 2000.

Mario Wienerroither, the mastermind behind the clip, took uncut footage from the video shoot (originally posted on YouTube in December 2009), and stripped out the music entirely. He then replaced it with an imagined sequence of noises created by Spears as she dances around in her famous skintight (and rather uncomfortable looking!) red bodysuit.

The sounds he creates ranges from the fireballs in the backdrop of Spears’ set to squeaks that accompany the pop diva’s movements in the leather catsuit. Wienerroither also includes imagined “hmphs” and “oomphs” from Spears as part of the hilarious new version.
