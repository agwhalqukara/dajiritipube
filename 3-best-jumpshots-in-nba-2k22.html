3 Best Jumpshots In NBA 2K22

There are many different jumpshots to choose from in the game, so it can be overwhelming to decide which one is the best for your playstyle.

3 Best Jumpshots in NBA 2K22

Here are three of the best jumpshots in NBA 2K22:

1. Ray Allen:

This jumpshot is smooth and consistent, making it a reliable choice for players of all skill levels. It has a medium release speed and a medium-high release point, which allows for a good balance of accuracy and power.

2. Stephen Curry:
