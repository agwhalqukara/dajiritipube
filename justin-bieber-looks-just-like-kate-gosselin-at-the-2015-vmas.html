Justin Bieber Looks Just Like Kate Gosselin at the 2015 VMAs

“What Do You Mean?” has a whole new meaning now. Justin Bieber showed up at MTV’s 2015 Video Music Awards at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles on Sunday, Aug. 30, sporting a fresh new ‘do that looked as if he were channeling Kate Gosselin’s famed pixie cut from 2009.

“I like the swoop,” host Miley Cyrus told the 21-year-old while opening the show. Bieber performed his hit new single for the first time on stage tonight.

The “Confident” singer, of course, has always been known for his hair. The former YouTube star made the hair flip his signature move when first breaking out in 2009, the same year that the Kate Plus Eight star debuted her hair style.
