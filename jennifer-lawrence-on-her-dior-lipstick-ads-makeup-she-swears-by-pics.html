Jennifer Lawrence on Her Dior Lipstick Ads, Makeup She Swears By: Pics

Her Capitol glam squad would be proud! Jennifer Lawrence is absolutely killing it as the stunning face of Dior’s Dior Addict Lipstick. But as Us Weekly discovered when we went behind the scenes of her video campaign shoot, the Hunger Games actress is just as down-to-earth as she seems—especially when it comes to her beauty routine.

When dishing on skincare, Lawrence, 25, told Us there’s no nonsense. “I wash my face, then moisturize,” she explained.

That said, her makeup bag is appropriately stocked. “I love mascara,” she confessed. As for her trick to always looking good on the go, the Joy actress shared, “I swear by the pink Dior Addict lipstick in ‘Smile.’ It’s quick and easy to apply in the spirit of ‘I don’t care, but don’t I look amazing?'”
