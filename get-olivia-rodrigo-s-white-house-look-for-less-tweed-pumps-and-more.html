Get Olivia Rodrigos White House look for less: Tweed, pumps and more

To quote one of her biggest hits, Olivia Rodrigo “looks happy and healthy” — and now she’s visited the White House.

The oh-so-popular “Good 4 U” singer was “honored” to stand beside President Joe Biden and Dr. Anthony Fauci to promote youth awareness of COVID-19 vaccinations Wednesday in the nation’s capitol.

“I’m in awe of the work President Biden and Dr. Fauci have done and was happy to help lend my support to this important initiative,” the 18-year-old pop superstar told the public. “It’s important to have conversations with friends and family members encouraging all communities to get vaccinated.”

Just as powerful as the “Sour” singer’s message was her pink Chanel power suit, platform pumps, and a miniature black purse to complete the look. Her girly girl and chic professional attire is the “Clueless” and “Gossip Girl” hybrid we’re all here for.

While you may not be joining President Biden for tea at the White House, Rodrigo’s trending sense of style can be replicated for a presentation at work or the next formal event in your planner. We researched, found the pop star’s exact pieces (minus the one-of-a-kind vintage suit, of course), and provided affordable and splurge-worthy dupes for more options.

And, unlike Rodrigo, you won’t need your “Driver’s License” — you can shop the blazers, pumps, purses, and more from the comfort of your home. Hurry, because everyone is thinking about tweed, platform pumps, and mini black bags after the press conference.

Olivia Rodrigo’s White House suit

The vintage Chanel suit isn’t sold, unfortunately, but here are similar styles. Shop more affordable options — starting at $38 — to higher-end blazers, skirts, and dresses up to $595.

Amanda Uprichard Shawl Collar Blazer, $286

The name of the game: tweed. Amanda Uprichard’s gorgeous Shawl Collar Blazer has a flattering fold and fit that can be worn with a black top or ruffled blouse for added texture.

Amanda Uprichard x REVOLVE Dixon Skort, $152 (originally $216)

The matching skirt from Amanda Uprichard is the preppy schoolgirl look you’ve been looking for, modernized from ’90s style.

L’AGENCE Alexandria Cropped Jacket, $595

Can we just say, this pink-colored jacket is chic, glam, and absolutely gorgeous? L’AGENCE is sold at REVOLVE and the black lining makes it great for a day in the office.

L’AGENCE Ashton Short, $295

L’AGENCE’s matching pink shorts are a dressier option for either work or a night out with friends. Not to mention, the striking color will garner compliments when you step out of your home.

Cinq a Sept Boucle Khloe Blazer, $495

Cinq a Sept Coronado Short, $207 (originally $295)

Lovers + Friends Tallulah Mini Dress, $175 (originally $218)
