Candace Cameron, Look-Alike Daughter Natasha Attend Teen Choice Awards

Seeing double? Candace Cameron Bure had a mini-me shadowing her on the red carpet at the 2014 Teen Choice Awards—her 15-year-old daughter, Natasha Valerievna Bure! 

The fresh-faced teen wore a floral gown while her mom stunned in a fitted white dress with tan heels. 

Not only did Candace take Natasha as her date, but she also scored her little girl a photo with one of the TCA's hottest stars, Ansel Elgort. 

"I just got mom of the year for this pic- @candacecbure with @anselelgort," Candace captioned a photo of her daughter with The Fault in Our Stars actor.
