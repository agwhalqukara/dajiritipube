Sophie Turner Reveals Her New Tattoos Are Fake

Sophie Turner is most famous for portraying Sansa Stark in the hit TV show Games Of Thrones. As a result, when she was spotted out and about in LA with a wolf tattoo on her arm, fans were left wondering whether it was related to the direwolves from the show.

Her other tattoos include a kiss mark on her collarbone and a rose and women in short skirts which accompany the wolf on her arm.

However, at Variety’s Young Power of Hollywood event, Sophie explained that the tattoos are for a new movie role. Therefore, they are only temporary tattoos and are in no way related to Game Of Thrones.

Sophie Turner will be playing the role of a young women called Josie in a new film called Huntsville. Turner will be starring alongside Dylan McDermott and Jack Kilmer. The movie is reportedly about a lonely man in Florida who enters a controversial relationship with a high school student. A release date is yet to be announced as filming has only just begun in Los Angeles.

This will be Sophie’s fourth movie role. Her latest, and arguably most famous, was as Jean Grey in X-Men Apocalypse. Whilst best known for her TV appearances, her other film work includes roles in Another Me and Barely Lethal.

Although naturally blonde, the actress is also known for her familiar ginger locks. Nevertheless, she has returned to her natural colour and dyed her hair blonde, which she debuted on Instagram 3 weeks ago, to become Josie.
