Claires New Job Leads To First Romance, Kyle Hires Her To Nanny Harrison?

The Young and The Restless spoilers and updates tease Claire Grace (Hayley Erin) will need a job and Kyle Abbott (Michael Mealor) will need a nanny for Harrison Abbott (Kellen Enriquez).

These two circumstances may work together for Claire to not only get a job as a nanny, but a first romance with Harrison’s father!

The Young And the Restless Spoilers: Claire Grace May Not Return To Being Her Grandmother’s Assistant

Once the Jordan (Colleen Zenk) mess is over with, it may not be good for Claire to return to the position she had when she was controlled by her.

That could bring up trauma for both her and her grandmother Nikki Newman (Melody Thomas Scott) whose assistant she was, although that’s not saying she might return to it later.

There likely would need to be a time of healing for both Claire and Nikki, not necessarily from each other but from the memories of that time.

Claire was a very good and competent assistant for Nikki but now that she’s been around children, Claire’s found she’s good for them and they’re good for her.

At the same time, Kyle’s nanny has had to take a leave of absence due to caring for her ill mother, and he’ll need someone to fill in.

Y & R Spoilers: Nanny Will Be Gone For Awhile

Traci Abbott (Beth Maitland) said she’d be happy to fill in for the nanny, but this could wind up being a permanent solution if her mother needs at home care.

Kyle will need someone at least long term temporary if not permanent to take care of little Harrison-he’s been from pillar to post enough. First he lost his mother and then Kyle and Summer Newman (Alison Lanier) divorced-and now a nanny he’s attached to has gone.

There is plenty of family support but Kyle will still need a nanny-fortunately, someone who loves children and is good with them who might want a job like that soon-Claire!

Claire and Summer are cousins since Nick Newman (Joshua Morrow) is her dad, and thanks to Victor Newman’s (Eric Braeden) plan to publicize Claire being Baby Eve the family now knows!

The Young And The Restless Spoilers: Claire Grace Has Never Dated

Claire obviously has never dated since Jordan raised her for revenge with no time for boys-and she’s pretty and blonde, like Summer.

Kyle burned his bridges when he cheated on Summer which may give Victoria Newman (Amelia Heinle) pause should Kyle hire Claire and then want to date her. Victor and the Newmans would likely give Kyle a good talking to that he’d better not hurt Claire like he hurt Summer!

Summer may even warn Claire once they get acquainted, but sometimes the heart wants what and who it wants, and a coupling up may happen anyway.

Hopefully Kyle has learned his lesson about waffling and if for no other reason, treat Claire right for little Harrison’s sake!
