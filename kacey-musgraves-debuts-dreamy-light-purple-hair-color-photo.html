Kacey Musgraves Debuts Dreamy Light Purple Hair Color: Photo

Embracing her inner unicorn! Kacey Musgraves hopped on Instagram on Tuesday, Aug. 18, to reveal that she switched up her hair color to one of the most mystical, magical shades: a soft lavender.

Musgraves’ shared pic features the “Follow Your Arrow” singer, a natural brunette, with her newly pastel tresses styled into curls. Her lush lashes are on display, as well as her teeny nose ring and rose-hued lips.

While the snap came sans caption, Musgraves did share a couple of sweet pics three days earlier that could have easily served as inspiration, including a cartoon pup and this one of a snow-white horse with blush hair:
